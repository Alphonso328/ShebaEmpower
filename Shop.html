Sheba is the Den Leader of SHeBA Women’s Empowerment Group which stands for She’s Better After it ALL. She lives it, eats it, sleeps it, laughs it ummm oh, and dances it. Its her life. She believes that she was born to empower women. Her life is orchestrated to do just that, literally everywhere she goes. She was born a leader but groomed to be a GREAT one, there is no such thing as a self-made womb-man, right? She understands that SHE IS because other women (and men) invested into to her with absolute (sometimes VERY annoying but NECESSARY) truth AND unconditional love. It would dishonor them by not being what they were to her to someone else (many others actually). Sheba is nothing more than a creative creator. She was blessed with creativity in order to create (produce, if you will). Other than empowerment, she educates. Just kidding, they’re actually the same thing. One of her favorite quotes is, “To empower is to educate and to educate is to empower.” If it relates to Education, its SHeBA. Bottom line. She is a Coach, Mentor, Entrepreneur, Author (1st Children’s book published in January 2020), Humanitarian and Anthropologist. She paints, writes music and poetry and SINGS like those damn 3 little birds pitch by the doorstep. She works closely with Youth too, one day looking to launch a mentorship program. Overall and in all, SHeBA will be the first to tell you that she loves God and doesn’t apologize or dumb her love and respect down for anyone. She also absolutely loves and adores people. Finally, she has always wanted her legacy to be unconditional love, therefore everything she does stems from such place.
